To Shape The Sleeve, Cast Stitch From One Nail At A Time Every Six Rows Of Knitting, First On One Side Of The Sleeve And Then On The Other, As Indicated On The Drawing. Then Every Five Rows, Every Four Rows, Every Three Rows, Every Two Rows, Until The Sleeve Is Long Enough (About 18 To 22 Inches) And Narrow Enough At The Cuff (About 8 Inches).
