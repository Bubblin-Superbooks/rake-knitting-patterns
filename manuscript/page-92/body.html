Border 14

Tan ground.

  • Tan ground. A—Chestnut-brown—fringe the same. B—Orange.
  • Champagne or fawn ground. A—Seal-brown—fringe the same. B—Burnt-orange, topaz, yellow-green or deep turquoise-blue.

Blue ground.

  • Green-blue ground. A—Dark yellow-green—fringe the same. B—Deep yellow-tan (chamois).
  • Peacock-blue ground. A—Black—fringe the same. B—Henna or burnt-orange.
